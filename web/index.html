sitl — disconnected

Controls

5.0 Hz

How often every drone broadcasts its state (position + velocity) to its peers, 0.1–10 Hz on a log slider. Between updates each drone runs a Kalman filter to predict where its peers are — lower the rate to save bandwidth and watch the prediction error grow. pause stops sharing entirely.

Hz

Give one drone its own share rate, independent of the fleet slider — e.g. throttle a single drone to 1 Hz (or 0.2 Hz for full drama) and watch its uncertainty circles grow on the map while the others stay tight.

start: arm, climb (30 m + 5 m per drone id) and fly a shared 40 m-radius orbit — altitude separation keeps it collision-free. stop: hold current position. land: auto-land and disarm.
